# 03 Design Direction

## Working Assumptions

- Brand name: AEROVOLT
- Product: premium smart cordless vacuum cleaner
- Audience: design-conscious urban households, pet owners, smart-home users
- Price tier: premium consumer technology
- Core qualities: quiet power, airflow engineering, lightweight control, sensor intelligence, clean home environment
- Touchpoints: product body mark, packaging, e-commerce product page, companion app, instruction manual, retail display, social ads

## Strategy

```text
design_direction: Precision Airflow Technology Identity
brand_positioning: premium smart cleaning technology for modern homes
target_audience: urban households that value clean design, reliable engineering, and quiet performance
visual_metaphor: controlled airflow, suction path, turbine precision, clean route, sensor intelligence
industry_style_logic: vacuum branding needs to communicate power and cleanliness without looking like a janitorial service or cheap appliance
aesthetic_reference: premium consumer electronics, Swiss product documentation, restrained smart-home UI language
geometry_system: circular airflow rings, negative-space A/V monogram, modular sensor dots, clean path lines
color_system: graphite black, cool white, electric cyan, soft silver, low-saturation blue gray
typography_system: refined geometric sans-serif, precise spacing, engineered but approachable
imagery_style: bright product photography, clean floor surfaces, subtle airflow visualization, no messy household scenes
application_logic: logo must work on product body, packaging, app icon, warranty card, retail display, and e-commerce thumbnails
avoid_list: cartoon vacuum icons, literal household clip art, excessive gradients, overdone cyberpunk, medical blue, generic SaaS symbol
```
