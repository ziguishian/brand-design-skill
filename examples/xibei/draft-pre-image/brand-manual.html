Northwest Food Identity System

朔禾

SHUOHE is a modern Northwest food brand built around grain, noodles, and pantry staples. The identity turns regional land, wheat, and bowl forms into a disciplined visual system.

Brand Philosophy

The brand avoids literal scenic Northwest imagery. Instead, it compresses dryland agriculture, warm grain, and the everyday food vessel into one simple mark that can scale from a package seal to a storefront sign.
