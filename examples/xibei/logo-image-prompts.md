# Logo Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts to generate the required logo exploration images before any SVG construction.

## Direction 1: Grain Horizon Bowl

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: logo exploration contact sheet for a Northwest Chinese food brand
Primary request: Create a vector-friendly logo exploration for a modern Northwest Chinese grain and noodle food brand named "朔禾 SHUOHE".
Style/medium: premium professional brand identity design, flat logo exploration, minimal geometry, no mockup, no 3D.
Subject: circular bowl symbol with a low horizon arc and three simplified grain stems, inspired by wheat fields and dryland agriculture in Northwest China.
Composition/framing: centered mark plus optional clean Chinese/Latin wordmark, strong negative space, scalable at small sizes.
Color palette: warm flour paper, deep ink black, millet gold accent, restrained clay red optional.
Typography direction: refined modern Chinese serif with quiet grotesk Latin support; if text appears, keep it clean and legible.
Constraints: must feel like a professional food packaging identity, regional but not touristy, premium everyday, production-ready.
Avoid: mountains, camels, ethnic pattern overload, vintage distress, cartoon wheat, generic organic leaf, fake 3D, packaging mockup, tiny unreadable text, watermark.
```

## Evaluation Criteria

Select the generated image direction that best satisfies:

- appetite and food credibility
- Northwest regional signal without cliché
- simple silhouette and strong small-size readability
- ability to become clean SVG
- packaging system potential
- professional, high-end design taste
