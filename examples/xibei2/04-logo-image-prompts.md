# 04 Logo Image Prompts

These prompts are the required image-generation step before SVG construction.

## Candidate 1: Field Bowl Mark

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: image-generated logo exploration, vector-friendly
Primary request: Create a professional logo exploration for a modern Northwest Chinese food brand named "原上禾 YUANSHANGHE".
Style/medium: premium brand identity design, flat vector-friendly logo exploration, clean studio presentation, no mockup, no 3D.
Subject: a circular bowl or plate form containing a simple field horizon line and one to three grain kernels; should feel like food from Northwest fields to the table.
Composition/framing: centered symbol with optional Chinese wordmark "原上禾" and small Latin "YUANSHANGHE"; strong negative space; readable at small size.
Color palette: warm flour paper, deep ink black, millet gold, small clay red accent.
Typography direction: refined modern Chinese serif or custom high-contrast Chinese wordmark, paired with quiet geometric Latin.
Constraints: professional designer-level, premium everyday food packaging, regional but not touristy, simple enough to reconstruct as SVG.
Avoid: mountains, camels, heavy ethnic patterns, distressed vintage texture, cartoon wheat, generic organic leaves, fake packaging mockup, watermark, tiny unreadable text.
```

## Evaluation Checklist

Before choosing a direction, evaluate:

- Does it look like food, not generic culture or tourism?
- Does it signal Northwest origin without obvious clichés?
- Is the silhouette strong at avatar size?
- Can it be reconstructed as clean SVG geometry?
- Can it extend into SKU colors, patterns, packaging backs, and social templates?
- Does it feel like professional design rather than AI decoration?
